#### 1.1 ğŸ¯ The Conviction Score Engine
**What**: AI-generated confidence rating (0-100) on whether a trade appears to be informed trading vs. coincidence.

## Part 7: Ethical Framework

### Principles

1. **All Data is Public**: Only use officially disclosed information
2. **Accuracy Over Sensationalism**: Every claim must be defensible
3. **Context Matters**: Always provide full context, not cherry-picked data
4. **Innocent Until Proven**: Scores indicate patterns, not guilt
5. **Transparency of Method**: All algorithms are explainable

### Disclaimers

Every page includes:
- "This is educational analysis, not legal accusation"
- "High scores indicate patterns worth investigating, not proof of wrongdoing"
- "All data from public STOCK Act disclosures"
- "Not financial advice"

### Legal Considerations

- First Amendment protected speech/journalism
- CDA Section 230 protections
- No defamation (stating facts and patterns only)
- Proper attribution of data sources
